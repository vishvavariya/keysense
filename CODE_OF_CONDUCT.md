# Code Of Conduct

KeySense should be a welcoming project for anyone who wants to learn from it, use it, or improve it.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially in code review.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and the work.
- Welcome beginners and explain tradeoffs clearly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or hostile review comments.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Spam or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block contributors when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
